FAQ

Common questions

Is booking direct actually cheaper than GetYourGuide?
+
Yes — verifiably. Speedboat: Rp 1.500.000 direct vs Rp 2.000.000 on GYG (save Rp 500.000). Waerebo: Rp 1.850.000 direct vs Rp 2.375.000 on GYG (save Rp 525.000). That saving comes directly from removing the OTA platform commission.
How do I pay?
+
Bank transfer (IDR/USD), credit/debit card, or cash on arrival for some experiences. A deposit secures your booking — we'll confirm payment details over WhatsApp. No hidden charges.
What's the cancellation policy?
+
Full refund for cancellations 7+ days before departure. 50% refund 3–6 days before. Within 3 days, we reschedule to available dates at no extra charge. Weather cancellations are always fully rescheduled or refunded — your safety is never negotiable.
Can I customise the itinerary?
+
Absolutely — this is the biggest advantage of booking direct. Private group? We adjust every stop. Special occasion? We'll arrange it. Specific spot you've read about? Let's talk. Just message us on WhatsApp with what you have in mind.
Are your boats licensed and safe?
+
Yes. All vessels are licensed by Indonesia's Ministry of Transport and operate under Komodo National Park regulations. Our crew hold certified safety training and every trip carries life jackets, first aid kits, and emergency communication equipment.
I'm travelling solo — can I still join?
+
Solo travellers are welcome on shared departures for the Speedboat Day Tour. For Phinisi and Waerebo, minimum is 2 pax — but WhatsApp us and we'll find you the best solution, including pairing you with open trip departures.
